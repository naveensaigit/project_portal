Get Started

STEPS to Get Started

Click on the get started button

Login/SignUp to the page

Compelete your profile if not completed

Float the projects if you wish to

Search for projects related to your interests on the dashboard

Explore more about a project by clicking on the link and entering the project page

Apply to the project and answer the question posed by the floater of the project to get selected

Work with great enthusiasm on the project after you get selected :)

About

What is UGAC Project Portal?

The UGAC Project Portal, as the name implies, is a centralised procedure for assigning projects to all interested undergraduate students. This allows them to work on projects that they are interested in inside their departments, as well as help initiate projects in their areas of interest. During the semester, it encourages students to participate in hands-on projects with IIT Mandi professors and their teams.
